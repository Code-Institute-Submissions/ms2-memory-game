Car Brands Memory Game

How To Play:

  • There are 16 cards in a 4x4 grid. Thera are 8 pairs of cards with the same symbol on them.
  • Click on a card to flip it.
    Then click on another one.
    If the second card have the same symbol on it like the one clicked before You found a match and the two cards will remain open.
    If not, the two cards will close again.
  • There is also a countdown timer wich starts at 60 seconds. So Time is also something to think about
  • The goal of the game is to match all pairs of cards before the time is up.

TIP: Go to the bottom of the screen where you can choose between day and night theme!
